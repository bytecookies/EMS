IMPORTANT NOTE FOR EXHIBITORS WITH CUSTOM BUILT BOOTHS

    The exhibitors and/or their contractors would not be permitted to undertake any substantial work such as carpentry, painting, welding, plastering, polishing and any other dust inducing work ath the venue.
