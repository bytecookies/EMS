IMPORTANT NOTE FOR EXHIBITORS WITH CUSTOM BUILT BOOTHS
  1. The exhibitors and/or their contractors would not be permitted to undertake any substantial work such as carpentry, painting, welding, plastering, polishing and any other dust inducing work ath the venue.
  2. Exhibitors and their contractors are advised to bring pre-fabricated booths which can be assembled and fitted with ease in the shortest possible time requiring only final finishing touches.

  4. Exibitors need to take prior approvals from the organisers before finalishing booth contractor/Stall design agency which is not listed in organisors approval list of vendors.
