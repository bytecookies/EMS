INTIMASIA - Exhibition Rules & Regulations

Definitions:

In this ‘Exhibition Rules & Regulations’ the following terms shall (except where the context otherwise require) have following meaning:

Applicant Means an individual, company or entity applying for participating in the exhibition & whose details are specified in the application.
Application Form Means the application form submitted by the applicant to apply for the right to participate in INTIMASIA, schedule to be held at the exhibition venue, a copy of which is attached with the exhibition rules & regulation.
Associates Means any of the following
  1. a) Any employee, contractors, subcontractors, representative or agents of the exhibitors or it associates
  2. b) In relation to an individual exhibitor means any relatives of the exhibitor & any company which the exhibitor or its relative is a shareholder or director of such company, and
  3. c) In relation to a corporate exhibitor means any shareholders or directors of the exhibitor and any relatives of such shareholders or directors and any company which the exhibitor or its shareholders or directors or any of their relatives is a shareholder or director of such company
Exhibition The exhibition to be known as INTIMASIA to be organized by the organizer at the exhibition venue during the exhibition period as specified in the application form
Exhibition Period The period mentioned in the application form attached along with the exhibition rules & regulations.
Exhibition Venue Means the exhibition center where the exhibition is going to be held
Exhibitor Means all employee & agents of any individual, applicant, sole proprietorship, partnership or limited company applying to exhibit at the exhibition and has been accepted in writing its application to exhibit at the exhibition by the organizers.
Organizer Peppermint Communications Pvt. Ltd.
Product Means any goods, products, item, sample or exhibits displayed by the exhibitor at the exhibition.
Publicity Material Means the promotional gift, catalogues & all, any advertising & publicity material whatsoever which an exhibitor wishes to distribute or use at the exhibition.
Bare/Raw Space Means the empty floor space.
Stand/Shell Booth Means exhibition stand and/or shell booth area within the space assigned to the exhibitor for the purpose of exhibiting and promoting the exhibitor produced. This area may include the structure partitioning of booth, as the case may be, but for the avoidance of doubt shall exclude any area in front of the booth.
Regulation The provision contained in this ‘Exhibition Rules & Regulations’

Organizers reserve the right to select participants exhibits to be displayed at its sole discretion. Until an exhibitor's application is accepted in writing by the organizer, no rights to exhibit are granted not withstanding payment or acceptance off the full rent submitted with the application All exhibitors must be legally registered companies in their country or origin or country of business. The Organizer may require exhibitors to produce a copy of their business registration certificate, certificate of incorporation or any other company registration document at any time. The space is licensed strictly to the exhibitor for trade promotion purpose only for the duration of the exhibition.

The applicant acknowledges that by signing the application / participation form, it has agreed & undertaken to comply with all its obligation under the any other additional rules & regulation as prescribed by the organizer.

Not with standing payment or acceptance of participation fee or part of thereof, submission of application form by the applicant to the organizer does not create whether express or implied, any rights on the part of the applicant any obligation or liabilities to perform any function or any duty on the part of the organizer.

The organizer has the sole and absolute discretion to accept or reject without reason or explanations, the applicant’s application to exhibit.


Participation Fees & Payment:

50% of the total participation amount should be included along with the Application Form for the booking to be accepted, with the balance 50% of the total amount should be paid in 2 instalments (i.e. 25% + 25%). The same to be paid 60 days and 30 days prior to event date. If the exhibitor cancels all or a part of the exhibit package / space contracted for hereunder 45 days prior, the exhibitor shall pay to the organizer a cancellation fee equal to 50% of the contract value. The organizer shall be entitled to 100% of the contract value in the event of cancellation by exhibitor. The organizer reserves the right to cancel the reservation of the space in the event of exhibitor not having paid dues of rental by due date. Payment already received will be forfeited in such cases. The organizer reserves the right to demand additional, non-interest-bearing deposit(s) at any time as the guarantee of the cost of actual or potential damage. In the event that the application is not accepted by the organizer, the fee paid shall be refunded without interest within 30 days from the date of notice of rejection.

Stand Space Allocation:

Allocation of space will be made by the organizer on ‘first –come-first-serve’ basis. Application duly on ‘first –come-first-serve’ basis. Application duly will only be considered for allocation of space. Before commencement of the exhibition period, the organizer will notify of the location & position of the stand.

The organizer has the sole and absolute discretion in allotting & specifying the location & position of the stand & reserve the right to re-allot & re-specify another stand different to the one originally allotted to the exhibitor at any time prior to the commencement of the exhibition period. For the avoidance of doubt, the exhibitor may not be subject to allocation or specification. All decision to such effect shall be final and no request for change shall be entertained. The right to use the stand allocated is personal to the exhibitor and may not be transferred, assigned, subcontracted, sub-let or otherwise shared with any third party.

Any exhibitor who is found by the organizer in its absolute opinion to have done so will be obliged to immediately withdraw from the exhibition, dismantle its stand and remove its exhibits at its own expense.

Location of Exhibit Space:

INTIMASIA will be held at venue as indicated in exhibition broucher, website and floor plan. All measurements and exhibit space layouts shown on the floor plan are as accurate as possible, but the organizers reserve the rights to make such modifications and changes to exhibit space assignments and to the location of the exhibit space as may be necessary to adjust the floor plan at any time, to meet the needs of the organizer, exhibitor and exhibit. The organizer reserves the option, in event of any emergency or other circumstances beyond its reasonable control, to either substitute comparable exhibit space or to refund the total rent paid hereunder.

Exhibitors’ Service Manual:

The organizer will furnish an ‘Exhibitors’ Service Manual’ prior to the exhibition. The exhibitor agrees to be bound by the rules and regulations as may be established from time to time by the organizer or as set forth in the exhibitor service manual. Complete shipping instructions and information regarding furniture rental, electrical work, telephone installation, plumbing, labor for erecting and dismantling exhibits, booths, specification of the labor requirements, labor rates, lighting, water, carpeting, internet access, food and beverage service, etc. will be contained in the Exhibitors Service Manual. A service desk will be maintained in the exhibit area.

Use of Stand:

Exhibitors stand must be manned by authorized & competent representative of the exhibitor at all times during the exhibition. Such representative must be fully conversant with the exhibitor’s products and / or services and shall be duly authorized to negotiate or conclude contracts for the sale of the exhibitor’s products & Services. The exhibitor shall produce confirmation that the representative shall duly comply with these conditions and with any & all directions which the organizer or its agents may give before or during the exhibition.

No advertising, demonstration or canvassing for business may be carried out anywhere else within the exhibition venue. No exhibits or advertising signs shall be placed outside the confines of the exhibitor’s stand. No stickers, posters, hangers, or other material shall be allowed to hang on fascia boards. No advertising or demonstration at the exhibition, including the staging of any fashion show, will be allowed at the exhibition venue unless prior approval in writing is obtained from the organizer. The use of laser products at the exhibition requires prior written permission from the organizers. Application of approval of such must be submitted to reach the organizer not later than one month before the exhibition opening. Public auctions shall not be permitted at the exhibition venue under any circumstances.

The exhibitor warrants that the exhibits & packages thereof & the publicity material or any other part of the display on the stand do not in any way whatever violate or infringe any 3 party rights including all intellectual property right including but not limited to trademarks, copyright, designs, names & patents whether registered or otherwise & agree to fully indemnify the organizer & its agents & contractors against all cost, expenses & damages arising from third party’s claim of infringements by the exhibitor any and/or the organizer of such third party’s right. The Complainant further agrees not to take any legal action or make any claim or demand against organisers for such violation or infringement.

No exhibitor shall engage in or permit filming, sound or video recording, telecasting and broadcasting at the exhibition venue unless prior written approval is obtained from the organizer.

The exhibitor will be responsible for the cleanliness and maintenance of their stalls. Cleaning of common passages/ areas will be arranged by the organizer. Facilities of outsourcing of cleaning services are available.

Participants are requested not to keep any publicity material outside the cubicle or above the height of the cubicle. Participants violating this are liable to a fine/penalty as decided by the event Sub Committee. Publicity and Promotion for the exhibition: The organizer shall arrange and be responsible for all the publicity arrangements for the exhibition both overseas & in India & no exhibitor or its agents shall give or cause to be given any interview, public announcement, press statement or any other publicity whatsoever intended to.
